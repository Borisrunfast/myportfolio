About Me

Hello! I’m Boris, a front-end developer based in Oslo. I specialize in building responsive web applications with a focus on smooth animations, performance, and user-friendly interfaces. My journey began with a fascination for pixel-perfect layouts and has grown into a passion for interactive experiences on the web.

When I’m not coding, you can find me stargazing, exploring hiking trails, or experimenting with the latest JavaScript frameworks. I thrive on collaboration and love working with teams that challenge me to learn new technologies.
